About Our College

Providing Quality Technical Education Since 1985

Govt. ITI College is dedicated to providing skill-based technical education to empower students for employment and self-reliance. Our institute focuses on practical training, discipline, and industry-ready skills.

Admission Process

Follow these simple steps to get admission in Govt. ITI College.

01

Check Eligibility

02

Online Registration

Apply through official ITI admission portal during admission dates.

03

Merit List

Admission is based on merit list released by the department.

Electrician

Fitter

Admission Procedure

Follow the Step-by-Step process for ITI admission.

1. Registration

Fill the application form online or offline

2. Document Verification

Submit 10th marksheet, Aadhaar, photo

3. Merit List

Check merit list & counseling result

4. Fee Payment

Pay the admission fees

5. Final Admission

Confirm seat & receive ID card

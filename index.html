About Our College

Providing Quality Technical Education Since 1985

Govt. ITI College is dedicated to providing skill-based technical education to empower students for employment and self-reliance. Our institute focuses on practical training, discipline, and industry-ready skills.

Govt. ITI College

Government Industrial Training Institute (ITI) is a premier technical education institute established to provide skill-based vocational training to students.

Our aim is to develop technically skilled manpower for industries, self-employment and national development.
